Health Voice Assistant User Manual

1. Introduction

The Health Voice Assistant is designed to assist users in managing their health using natural language voice commands. It provides quick access to health information, personalized recommendations, and helpful features such as appointment scheduling and medication reminders.

Key Features

  • Voice-activated health information retrieval
  • Personalized health recommendations based on user history
  • Easy appointment scheduling and reminders
  • Medication reminders for timely doses

2. Getting Started

System Requirements

To use the Health Voice Assistant, ensure that your device meets the following requirements:

  • Compatible web browser (Chrome, Firefox, Safari, etc.)
  • Working microphone
  • Internet connection

Accessing the Health Voice Assistant

  1. Open your web browser and navigate to [website-url].
  2. Click on the "Start Listening" button to activate the voice assistant.

Enabling Microphone Access

The Health Voice Assistant requires access to your device's microphone. Make sure to grant the necessary permissions when prompted.

3. Using the Voice Assistant

Activation

To activate the voice assistant, click the "Start Listening" button. Once activated, you'll see a visual indication that the assistant is ready to receive voice commands.

Asking Questions

Speak clearly and concisely to ask health-related questions or give commands. For example, say "What are the symptoms of flu?" or "Schedule an appointment for tomorrow."

Command Examples

  • "Tell me about [health topic]."
  • "Any recommendations for a healthy diet?"
  • "Schedule an appointment with Dr. Smith on [date and time]."
  • "Remind me to take my medication at [time]."
